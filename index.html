Our intention is to aggregate health inspection reports and make them available via our website, mobile application and freely via our API. Subscribers will receive alerts when the an inspection has been detected for their favorite eating establishment. Subscribers will also get access to historic inspection results when available.

Democratic "Code-Sourcing"

  • Anyone can consume the API free of charge.
  • Anyone can help populate our database with inspection results from their region.
  • Bounties can be awarded by people who want results from their region.
  • Vote for the regions that you want to see inspections for.
  • As a side effect restaurants and food premises will be kept accountable.

What if my location isn't listed?
If your region or municipality has a publicly accessible website of inspection result data, and it isn't currently represented in our results, then you can request to have your regions added to the list of crawled sites.
